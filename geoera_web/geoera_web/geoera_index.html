About Us

One of the greatest challenges facing humanity is natural disasters that cannot be avoided. Although the types and severity of disasters vary according to their geography, the most common ones are earthquake, flood, erosion, fire and volcanic eruptions. Today, the amount of loss of life and property damage caused by natural disasters has decreased significantly thanks to the increasing social awareness and technological developments regarding these events. One of the biggest disasters that needs to be taken care of is the dam floods that have occurred in many places recently and cause significant loss of life and economic assets. Most flooding in dams is based on soil structure sliding from the dam reservoir to the embankment dams. We, as GEOERA, are examining the shifts (deformations) in dam reservoirs. And we give information about how much it will slide in the future.
